Savory Avocado Toast


Jump To Recipe

Background

Avocado toast has generally been regarded as more modern food, served in city cafes and breakfast nooks. But, the food item still provides plenty of good tasting
Fiber, Healthy Fats, and Nutrients. it's very simple and easy to make for those in a rush during the morning hours. It's also very open to additional ingredients and
seasonings, making it easily personalizable and able to appeal to anybody's taste. In short, it's an incredibly easy, healthy, and tasty breakfast option for those
with any type of schedule or taste.

Optional Flair

One of the benefits of a seemingly plain meal like avocado toast is that it can be easily added to. Many foods and spices can easily be put onto the slice of toast
making it easily curated to anyone's tastes. It can be made spicy, salty, and anything and everything in between. As has been said earlier, you can put any different topping
onto the toast, but here are some toppings that i've found to go well with avocado toast.

  • Everything Bagel Seasoning
  • Old Bay Seasoning
  • Any Hot Sauce/Seasoning, For the daring
  • Simple Salt & Pepper
  • An Egg

These are just some suggestions, they're not requirements or anything similar. You can ad-lib with almost anything in your pantry and the toast will still taste good.

Recipe

Prep Time: ~10 Min

Serves 1 Person

Ingredients

  • 1 Slice of any type of bread. Sourdough and Whole Grain bread are suggestions
  • 1/2 of a fresh avocado
  • Salt & Pepper

Directions

  1. Toast the slice of bread to your preferences, Start preparing any flair/toppings
  2. Cut the avocado in half and remove the pit, if necessary. Store the other half for later use.
  3. Using a spoon, empty the contents of the avocado into a small bowl.
  4. Using either a masher or tines of a fork, mash the avocado into a rough paste.
  5. Spread paste along peice of bread, top with salt and pepper and any other flair

It really is that simple, but so tasty and elegant. Again, I recommend that you top this with anything your heart desires. With this, i'm almost certain you'll feel energized
and excited for your day.
